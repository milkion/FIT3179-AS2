Drug Addiction in Malaysia

Drug addiction in Malaysia has been a significant public health concern for decades. Despite stringent laws and ongoing efforts to combat drug abuse, the country continues to grapple with this complex issue. The data presented here offers a comprehensive look at the patterns and trends of drug addiction across different states, drug types, age groups, and genders from 2000 to 2021.

Through this visualization, we aim to unravel the intricate story of drug addiction in Malaysia. Each section is designed to provide insights into the multifaceted nature of this issue, encouraging viewers to delve deeper into the data and understand the underlying factors contributing to these trends.
